Red Sangria


Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle Merlot/Malbec
  • 1/4 Cup Triple Sec
  • 1/4 Cup Brandy
  • 2 Tbsp Lime Juice
  • 2 Tbsp Orange Juice
  • Apples, Oranges, Peaches, etc for Garnish

Recipe

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large pitcher
